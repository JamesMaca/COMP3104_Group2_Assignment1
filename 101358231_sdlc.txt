Learning DevOps engineering is crucial in the software development cycle for several important reasons
Efficiency and Speed: DevOps practices emphasize automation and collaboration between development and operations teams. 
This leads to faster development cycles, quicker releases,
and reduced time-to-market for software products. Learning DevOps helps streamline processes,
 reducing manual work and bottlenecks and many more.
